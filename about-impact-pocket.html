Impact Pockets Is Dedicated To Supporting Experienced Professionals In Launching Their Own Funds. We Only Elect To Work With Experienced Management Teams

Discover Who We Are

Impact Pockets helps proven professionals launch their own private equity funds with purpose. We partner exclusively with experienced operators who have built, scaled, or exited successful businesses. Through a comprehensive incubation model, we provide everything from fund architecture and compliance to investor outreach, AI-integrated workflows, and tax optimization. Our mission is to remove friction from fund creation and empower you to transform your real-world expertise into an investable vehicle that delivers long-term value and measurable impact. We believe the next generation of private equity leaders aren’t waiting for someone else to write the check they’re stepping up to write their own. You’ve already done the hard part. You know what works. Now it’s time to scale your influence and build a legacy fund backed by systems, strategy, and support.

Lane Campbell, Managing Partner

Lane Campbell is a serial founder and innovation strategist known for launching AI companies in partnership with MIT professors and helping open the NASDAQ. He has led ventures across GovTech, compliance, and international business, bringing a rare mix of technical expertise and operational insight. Lane supports partners in designing scalable systems, leveraging emerging technologies, and executing fund strategy with precision and speed.

Chris Lemire, CPA, Managing Partner

Chris Lemire is a Certified Public Accountant with decades of experience advising high-net-worth families on international tax, estate planning, and business holdings. His deep understanding of global structuring and risk management has helped countless clients optimize wealth and minimize liability. At Impact Pockets, Chris guides fund managers through the financial and legal architecture needed to build sustainable, high-performing funds.
